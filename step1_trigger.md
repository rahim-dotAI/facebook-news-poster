\# Step 1: Trigger (Schedule)



\*\*Type:\*\* Schedule Trigger  

\*\*Cron Expression:\*\* `0 \*/2 \* \* \*`  

\*\*Description:\*\* Runs at 0 minutes past the hour, every 2 hours (UTC).

}
